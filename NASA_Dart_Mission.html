What has it taught me ?

The process of developing a new technology has provided invaluable insights. Transforming an idea into a functional product is a meticulous endeavor that cannot be accomplished overnight. It requires a careful and iterative approach, accompanied by thorough documentation, to mitigate numerous variables and uncertainties. This deliberate approach fosters the creation of robust intellectual property, guaranteeing a comprehensive understanding of the development process and the methods employed.
